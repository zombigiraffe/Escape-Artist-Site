"Gravity" released on December 18th 2022, is our fourth recorded single produced by Hayden Calhoun at Darkside Audio. This release is accompanied by our 2nd official music video produced by Sarah Novroski of Novro Studios. Gravity was written and collaborated to achieve relaying a straight forward, and hard hitting message. With lyrics reflecting the weight of world conflict and environmental turmoil, there's hardly a name so fitting. Visit our home page to listen.

We released our debut EP, "Memories" July 5th 2021, Mixed and Mastered by Eric Novroski at Novro Studios. This EP contains 6 tracks and features our friend Orion Stephens from In Dying Arms. "Memories" EP is available on YouTube Music, Spotify, Pandora, and Apple Music. Physical copies are available through contact on our social media pages, and are available at any show we play.

"Memories" is our third released single and is the title track to our debut EP. This track also features a music video filmed by Sarah Novroski of Novro Studios, and can be found on our YouTube channel Linked in our YouTube Shortcut icon.

"A Thousand Miles" is our second released single and is also featured in our "Memories" EP. This track features a visualizer video on our YouTube channel put together by Niko Giranda (ex. Hopewell Furnace) at NikoFreakshow Entertainment.

"Forever" is our first single recorded with Lee Fenstamaker at his home studio and was our debut into the music scene.
