We released our debut EP July 5th 2021, Mixed and Mastered by Eric Novroski at Novro Studios. This EP contains 6 tracks and features our friend Orion Stephens from In Dying Arms. "Memories" EP is available on YouTube Music, Spotify, Pandora, and Apple Music. Physical copies are available through contact on our social media pages, and will soon be featured on our "Store" tab here on our website.

"Memories" is our third released single and is the title track to our debut EP. This track also features a music video filmed by Sarah Novroski of Novro Studios, and can be found on our YouTube channel Linked in our YouTube Shortcut icon.

"A Thousand Miles" is our second released single and is also featured in our "Memories" EP. This track features a visualizer video on our YouTube channel put together by Niko Giranda (ex. Hopewell Furnace) at NikoFreakshow Entertainment.
